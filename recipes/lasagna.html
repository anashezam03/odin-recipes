Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's a hearty, comforting meal perfect for family gatherings and special occasions.



Ingredients:
  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 Pound Ground Beef
  • 1 Medium Onion, Finely Chopped
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 Cups Ricotta Cheese
  • 2 Cups Shredded Mozerrella
  • 1 Cup Grated Parmesean
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 4 Cups Marinara Sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 2 Tablespoons Chopped Fresh Basil
  • 2 Tablespoons Chopped Fresh Parsley
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Black Pepper
How to cook:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions; drain.
  3. Cook beef in a skillet until browned; drain fat.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan in a bowl.
  5. Layer in a 9x13-inch baking dish: sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, beef, mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with remaining mozzarella.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Take out of the oven once done, garnish with herbs/seasoning of choice.
  9. Let sit for 10 minutes before enjoying!
