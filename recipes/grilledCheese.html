Grilled Cheese

Grilled cheese is a delicious dish that can be served at any time!



Ingredients:
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Mayonaise (Trust me)
  • Cheese


Steps:
  1. Heat up pan on medium heat.
  2. Spread Mayonaise on both sides of each loaf of bread.
  3. Make a sandwich with the cheese in the middle of both slices of bread.
  4. Place the sandwich on the pan once its hot. Leave for around 2-3 minutes or until the bread is golden, then flip.
  5. Cook the other side of the sandwich. Cover and wait until golden-brown. Remove from pan safely and place on a plate.
  6. Enjoy!
