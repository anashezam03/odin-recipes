Omelette

Omelette is a very simple yet delicious dish. You only need 3 ingredients to create the dish.



Ingredients:
  • 2 Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Spices


Steps:
  1. Pre-heat your pan on medium heat.
  2. While pan is heating, crack eggs into a bowl, beat them together and season with any seasoning you enjoy.
  3. Pour olive oil on the pan, once oil appears to look less viscous moving around the pan, pour the eggs into the pan.
  4. Do not touch the omelette for around 4 minutes. Wait for the base of the omelette to cook. A crust will form to show it's cooked.
  5. Once the base is cooked, fold the omelette over. Leave until omelette is fully cooked. Then take of the pan and serve.
